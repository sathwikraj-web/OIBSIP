Biography

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, affectionately known as "Captain Cool", is one of India's most iconic cricketers and former captain of the Indian national team. Known for his calm demeanor and sharp cricketing mind, Dhoni led India to numerous historic victories including the 2007 T20 World Cup, 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup, and the 2013 Champions Trophy.

Born in Ranchi, Jharkhand, Dhoni rose from humble beginnings to become a global sports legend. His finishing skills, leadership qualities, and ability to stay composed under pressure earned him worldwide respect.

Beyond cricket, Dhoni is an inspiration to millions and a symbol of hard work, discipline, and perseverance.
